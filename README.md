# 🚗 Car Sales Analysis Using Python

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing car sales data using Python to uncover trends, customer preferences, pricing patterns, and market dynamics. Through Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), the project provides insights into factors influencing vehicle sales, including brand popularity, fuel preferences, transmission types, pricing, and geographical distribution.

The goal is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that can support strategic decision-making in the automotive industry.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze car sales trends and pricing patterns.
* Identify top-performing brands and vehicle segments.
* Understand customer preferences for fuel types and transmissions.
* Examine the impact of vehicle age and mileage on pricing.
* Explore regional market distribution.
* Generate actionable insights through data visualization.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Brand Analysis

* Premium brands such as BMW, Audi, and Range Rover have the highest average vehicle prices.
* Popular mass-market brands dominate overall sales volume.

### Fuel Type Analysis

* Petrol vehicles account for the largest share of sales.
* Electric and Hybrid vehicles have higher average prices compared to conventional fuel types.

### Pricing Analysis

* Most vehicles fall within the mid-price segment.
* Newer vehicles generally command higher prices.

### Ownership Analysis

* First-owner vehicles retain greater market value.
* Resale prices decline as the number of owners increases.

### Market Analysis

* Vehicle sales are concentrated in a few major states.
* Regional differences influence pricing and demand patterns.

---

## 📉 Correlation Findings

* Vehicle price decreases with increasing mileage.
* Vehicle price decreases as car age increases.
* Age and mileage together strongly influence resale value.
* Newer vehicles tend to maintain higher market prices.
